## Hex.pm Security Audit Progress

The Hex.pm security audit is well underway, with significant progress made this
month.

### Audit Status

* **zentrust partners GmbH** has completed their security assessment
* **Paraxial.io** has started their audit engagement

### Initial Findings

The completed audit identified the following issues:

* **3** high severity issues
* **1** medium severity issues
* **18** low severity issues
* **2** info findings

_Note: These findings are still being triaged. The final issue count and
severity classifications may change as we complete the review process._

### Remediation Work

We have begun remediation work to address the identified vulnerabilities.

#### CVE-2026-21618: XSS in OAuth2 Device Grant Auth Screen

The first public fix addresses an XSS (Cross-Site Scripting) vulnerability
discovered in the OAuth2 Device Grant authentication screen. During
remediation, we conducted a broader review of the codebase to identify similar
patterns that could potentially lead to XSS vulnerabilities and addressed those
as well.

* [CVE-2026-21618](https://cna.erlef.org/cves/CVE-2026-21618.html)
