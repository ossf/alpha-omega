# OpenRefactory Update: July 2025

* Project kickoff meeting was held on July 9
* Two milestones to be completed in August. (1) Milestone 1: Dependency Graph and SBOM Workflows, due on Aug 22, 2025 and (2) Milestone 2: Java Call Graph Generator, due on Aug 29, 2025
* The call graph output should be in a canonical format for method/function naming. We had meetings with the caps lock team about this. But no specific decision has been made. OpenRefactory sat with Sergej to set up a format to follow based on javap specification. This will be followed now. Note, Rust Foundation is also addressing the issue to have a canonical format. 
* OpenRefactory provided two VEX informations to Piotr: one for CVE-2025-48924 and one for CVE-2024-51504. In both cases, the CVEs were not reachable.
* VEX Initiative disclosed on Solr public dev@solr mailing list. The team generated a VEX document that was requested by Apache Solr. The work is acknowledged by the Apache Solr team: https://lists.apache.org/thread/230mfp5f65nf4m2r215yfrn4kblf4r01
